Course Vision課程願景
"AI is not a side skill reserved for technical teams — it is a core business capability." 「AI 不再只是技術團隊的專屬工具，而是商業人才的核心能力之一。」

This curriculum helps students evolve from passive AI users into AI-native builders who can identify real demand, design the right solution, and deliver value quickly. Every module connects technical possibilities to clear business outcomes. 本課程幫助學生由 AI 被動使用者，成長為能識別真實需求、設計可行方案、快速完成驗證的AI 原生商業建構者。每個模組均將技術可能性與明確的商業產出緊密連結。

Three Pillars of the Curriculum課程三大支柱

Three Frontiers,
One Unified Business Lens 三大前沿方向
統一商業視角

Each pillar targets a distinct dimension of modern AI — aligned to real business problems and taught through hands-on building. 每個支柱針對現代 AI 的不同維度，圍繞真實商業問題，以動手實踐為核心教學方式。

PRED
Pillar 01支柱 01
Predictive AI預測式 AI

Using traditional machine learning and LLMs to solve prediction tasks — matching problems to the right method and translating outputs into better business decisions. 運用傳統機器學習與 LLM 完成預測任務——為問題匹配合適的方法，並將結果轉化為更好的業務決策。

Forecasting ML vs LLM Decision Support 預測 ML 對比 LLM 決策支持
PERC
Pillar 02支柱 02
Perceptive AI感知式 AI

How AI perceives the world through multimodal information — practical OCR and NLP, plus hands-on auditing cases that show how perception-driven AI solves real challenges. AI 如何通過多模態資訊感知世界——實踐 OCR 與 NLP，並通過審計案例展示感知驅動 AI 如何解決真實挑戰。

OCR & NLP Document AI Information Extraction OCR & NLP 文件 AI 資訊提取
GEN
Pillar 03支柱 03
Generative AI生成式 AI

Leveraging business students' strengths to translate market needs into product ideas — rapid no-code prototyping of AI demos for early concept validation. 發揮商科學生優勢，將市場需求轉化為產品機會——以零代碼工具快速原型驗證商業想法。

No-Code Prototyping Market-First Rapid MVP 零代碼原型 市場優先 快速 MVP
Pedagogical Approach教學方法

Learning by Building做中學

We remove unnecessary technical barriers and keep students focused on business value creation from day one. 我們移除不必要的技術障礙，從第一天起讓學生聚焦於商業價值創造。

01

Low-Code / No-Code Empowerment低代碼 / 零代碼賦能

Build functional AI solutions without needing deep software engineering expertise.無需深厚的軟件工程背景，即可構建功能完整的 AI 解決方案。

02

Market-First Design市場優先設計

Start with customer pain points and unmet demand — not with tools or technology.從客戶痛點和未滿足的市場需求出發，而非從工具或技術出發。

03

Rapid Prototyping快速原型驗證

Convert ideas into testable MVPs in weeks, not semesters. Validate assumptions early.以週為單位將想法轉化為可測試的 MVP，盡早驗證關鍵假設。
